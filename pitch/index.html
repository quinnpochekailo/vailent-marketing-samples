Foundation. Generation.
Conversion. Analysis.

Step 1 · Foundation
Knowledge base + research
Verified KB. Founder and customer interviews captured. Product walkthroughs, technical references, and a steady signal feed from the trade press. The credibility gate — nothing publishes until this is locked.
→
Step 2 · Generation
Cutting-edge AI content
Visually stunning playbooks, videos, interviews, long-form essays — built on the verified KB, tuned to each persona. Content that spurs conversation, not filler.
→
Step 3 · Conversion
Persona-optimized landing pages
Top-of-the-line landing pages built on Step 1's KB + Step 2's content. Core Web Vitals green, schema-tagged, A/B variants from day one. Each page maps to one persona, one angle, one offer.
→
Step 4 · Analysis
Closed-loop automation
Attribution feeds back into Step 1. The hub learns what converts, retires what doesn't, and scales the winners. Decision Records on every shipped artifact — fully auditable.

Each step compounds the next. The hub gets sharper with every cycle — every decision backed by data.
